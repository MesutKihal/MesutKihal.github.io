Projects

Almaarefa

Almaarefa (almaarefa.site) is a specialized Algerian educational platform designed to support students and teachers throughout the national curriculum, It serves students of all ages, from primary and middle school to secondary (high school), as well as those preparing for national exams like the BEM and BAC.

Art & Culture

Art & Culture (artblog.onrender.com) is a dedicated Algerian digital magazine that serves as a vibrant cultural hub for the arts in Algeria. Based in Ben Aknoun, Algiers, it provides a modern platform to showcase the country's rich creative landscape.
